Staysure Travel Insurance: How to Get a Family Discount

  1. Home
  2. Blog
  3. Blog Details

Traveling with family is one of life’s greatest joys, but it also comes with its fair share of uncertainties. From flight cancellations to unexpected medical emergencies, having reliable travel insurance is no longer a luxury—it’s a necessity. Staysure Travel Insurance offers comprehensive coverage tailored for families, and the best part? You can save big with their family discount. Here’s everything you need to know to secure the best deal while keeping your loved ones protected.

Why Family Travel Insurance Matters More Than Ever

In today’s fast-changing world, travel risks have multiplied. Geopolitical tensions, climate-related disruptions, and lingering health concerns make it crucial to have a safety net. A single unexpected event can turn a dream vacation into a financial nightmare.

Rising Travel Costs and Financial Protection

With inflation driving up travel expenses, losing money due to cancellations or delays can hit families hard. Staysure’s policies cover trip interruptions, lost baggage, and even emergency evacuations, ensuring you don’t bear the full brunt of unforeseen costs.

Health Concerns Post-Pandemic

While the worst of the pandemic may be behind us, new variants and regional outbreaks still pose risks. Staysure includes medical coverage abroad, so you’re not left scrambling for expensive healthcare in a foreign country.

How Staysure’s Family Discount Works

Staysure understands that insuring multiple family members can get expensive. Their family discount makes it affordable without compromising on coverage.

Who Qualifies for the Discount?

Typically, the discount applies to policies covering at least two adults and two children (or one adult and three children). Some key eligibility criteria include:
- All travelers must reside at the same address.
- Children are usually defined as under 18 (or under 23 if in full-time education).
- The policy must cover everyone for the same trip duration.

How Much Can You Save?

Discounts vary but often range from 10% to 20% off the total premium. For larger families, the savings can be substantial.

Steps to Claim Your Family Discount

Getting the discount is straightforward if you follow these steps:

1. Gather Your Family’s Travel Details

Before applying, have the following ready:
- Full names and ages of all travelers.
- Trip dates and destinations.
- Any pre-existing medical conditions (Staysure specializes in covering these).

2. Use the Online Quote Tool

Staysure’s website lets you input your family’s details and instantly see discounted rates. Be sure to:
- Select the "Family" option when prompted.
- Adjust coverage levels based on your needs (e.g., higher medical limits for adventure travel).

3. Review the Policy Inclusions

Not all family discounts are created equal. Double-check:
- Does it cover all destinations on your itinerary?
- Are activities like skiing or scuba diving included?
- What’s the claims process like?

4. Apply Early for the Best Rates

Prices often rise as your departure date approaches. Booking at least a month in advance can lock in lower premiums.

Maximizing Your Coverage Without Overspending

A discount is great, but you also want to avoid over-insuring. Here’s how to strike the right balance.

Skip Redundant Coverage

If your credit card already offers rental car insurance, don’t pay extra for it. Similarly, check whether your health insurance extends overseas.

Consider Annual Policies for Frequent Travelers

If your family travels more than twice a year, an annual plan might be cheaper than multiple single-trip policies—even with the discount.

Real-Life Scenarios Where Staysure Saved Families

Case 1: Last-Minute Cancellation Due to Illness

The Thompson family had to cancel their European tour when their youngest contracted chickenpox. Thanks to Staysure’s cancellation coverage, they recouped 100% of their non-refundable expenses.

Case 2: Emergency Medical Evacuation in Asia

When Mr. Rodriguez suffered a heart attack in Thailand, Staysure arranged and covered his medical flight back to the U.S., saving his family over $50,000.

Common Mistakes to Avoid When Buying Family Travel Insurance

Assuming All Providers Offer Family Discounts

Many insurers charge per person, making family travel insurance prohibitively expensive. Always compare providers.

Overlooking Pre-Existing Conditions

Staysure is one of the few insurers that covers pre-existing conditions without exorbitant fees. Disclose everything upfront to avoid claim denials.

Ignoring Regional Risks

If you’re traveling to hurricane-prone areas or regions with political unrest, ensure your policy includes natural disaster and political evacuation coverage.

Final Tips for a Stress-Free Family Vacation

  • Keep a digital copy of your policy accessible to all family members.
  • Save Staysure’s 24/7 emergency helpline in your phone.
  • Teach older kids basic insurance info (e.g., what to do if they lose their passport).

With the right preparation and Staysure’s family discount, you can focus on making memories—not worrying about "what ifs." Safe travels!
